New Graduate Program

Here at Infinite Therapist we understand what it may feel like to be a new graduate who is excited about beginning a new career in therapy, yet being overwhelmed in the process of matching your current skill set to jobs. Yes, we were once there too! That is why we offer mentorship based travel therapy positions that allow you to gain clinical experience in various settings across the U.S. Travel therapy through our new graduate program will allow you an opportunity to gain experience in various work settings at an accelerated rate while receiving top compensation. You will have the support of a therapist to guide you in your new career so that you never feel alone. That’s just one of many amenities we offer our new graduates. The travel therapy incentives are endless!

What We Do

At Infinite Therapist we specialize in meeting the needs of our therapist and clients by providing contract, contract to hire, direct hire job placement and staffing solutions on a global scale. As a therapist owned and operated company we understand and know how to support the unique balance between the needs of healthcare facilities and the career interest of our therapist.
