Staffing

We recognize that changes in your staffing needs can happen quickly and without notice. The need to fill those positions can have a negative impact on your organization and patient care/outcomes. We also understand that return on investment is what matters when choosing a therapy staffing company to assist you with long/short term coverage. Therefore, our solutions at the basic level save you time and money. We offer the following staffing solutions:

Contract Staffing:

Staffing solution for short term coverage that can be customized to meet your organization's immediate needs.

Contract to Hire:

For employers who want to have a trial period with a therapist to ensure they are a match to the job and culture of your organization.

Permanent Placement:

Staffing solution for employers that are looking for a therapist to employ on a long term permanent basis.

Our simplified process that will save you time and money:

  • Schedule a consultation to determine your staffing needs.
  • A Therapy consultant will submit resumes to you of pre-screened applicants.
  • Let us know which applicants meet your needs and we will set up a quick and easy interview.
  • Have a competent therapist giving the best care to your clients.

Retaining therapy talent for your organization and the relationships we build in the therapy community will make Infinite Therapist a true valued partner. If hiring a therapist is a priority for your company, we would love to speak to you.

Have questions? Ready to get started?

Schedule a consultation to speak with a therapy recruitment consultant who is eager to learn more about you, your company and your hiring needs. It is our pleasure to assist you with your hiring needs for today and in the future.
