Career

Our goal is to assist you in finding the perfect therapy job. We will work in partnership with you to discover what your career goals are and develop a plan to accomplish just that! We have a simplified process to get you started whether you are looking for contract, contract to hire or permanent career opportunities:

Contract Therapy:

For therapist looking for exciting short 13 week assignments; Can be local or travel.

Contract to Hire:

For therapists looking to try a position on a contractual basis before becoming permanent.

Permanent Placement:

For therapist looking to settle down with a permanent position in a desired setting or location.

Our simplified process to get your career going:

  • Schedule a consultation to determine your career goals.
  • Our therapy consultant will create your profile and send your resume to potential companies that best meets your expectations.
  • We will then contact you to schedule an interview.
  • If the company is a good fit you will be on your way to a successful and satisfying career!

Have questions? Ready to get started?

Schedule a consultation to speak with a therapy recruitment consultant who is eager to learn more about you and your career interest.
